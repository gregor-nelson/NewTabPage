# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2025

## Data Collection

This extension collects no data. Zero.

## Data Storage

Your settings (time format, calendar preferences) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

## External Services

The extension loads fonts from Google Fonts. No tracking or personal data is sent to Google.

## Permissions

The extension requests only one permission:
- **Storage**: To save your settings locally

## Changes

Any changes to this policy will be reflected here with an updated date.
